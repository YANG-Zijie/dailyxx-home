### ğŸŒŸ App Overview

This app is inspired by a fun linguistic observation:

"Did you XX today?"

Replace "XX" and almost any action or habit can fit the sentence.

For example:
Did you study today?

It covers a surprisingly wide range:
Positive/negative actions:
Did you wake up early today?
Did you stay up late today?

Active/passive actions:
Did you work overtime today?
Did you get forced to work overtime today?

Self vs. others:
Did you check in with your mother today?
Did your son eat well today?

...

Even more interesting: swap the question mark "?" for an exclamation mark "!" and the question smoothly turns into a statement that logs today's status:
Did you study today? -> You study today!
Did you slack off today? -> You slack off today!

With a single punctuation change, it becomes a light, fun way to remind, encourage, or warn.

Also, every time you check in with "You XX today!", the app auto-generates a shareable screenshot. Share it with friends so you can keep each other accountable.

Give it a try. Let "Did you XX today?" sweep through 2025! Let's discover more fun combinations together:

Did you XX today?
You XX today!
